Our Work

What Do We Do?

Funding Urgent Theatre Operations

We cover the costs needed for quick surgical interventions like VP shunt insertions and ETV procedures. This ensures that no newborn waits too long for life-saving surgery and reduces the risk of complications.

Supporting Medical Teams

We provide overtime allowances for doctors and nurses who operate after normal working hours. This support prevents delays and ensures medical teams can respond immediately to emergencies.

Supplying Essential Surgical Materials

We help ensure that VP shunts, theatre consumables, and emergency supplies are available when needed. Adequate resources allow hospitals to perform surgeries efficiently and safely.

Assisting Families

We offer guidance, counselling, and follow-up support to parents during and after the treatment process. Families receive information, emotional support, and help navigating hospital procedures.
