We support newborn babies with Hydrocephalus by funding critical theatre operations and helping medical teams perform life-saving procedures on time.

Introduction

Hydrocephalus Theatre Funding Organization is a health-support initiative that helps newborn babies in Malawi receive immediate life-saving surgery for Hydrocephalus. We partner with medical teams to reduce waiting times, provide essential supplies, and fund after-hours operations so no child suffers or dies because of delayed treatment.
