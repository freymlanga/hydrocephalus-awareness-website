About Us

Who Are We?

We are a community-driven organization dedicated to helping newborn babies diagnosed with Hydrocephalus receive quick and safe surgical treatment. Our work started at Queen Elizabeth Central Hospital, where we supported urgent theatre interventions for infants who could not wait long for treatment.

Our Mission

To reduce disability and mortality caused by Hydrocephalus by funding timely theatre operations, supporting medical teams, and ensuring essential surgical materials are available.

Why Our Work Matters

Hydrocephalus is a serious condition where excess fluid builds up in a baby’s brain. When treatment is delayed, it leads to severe brain damage, disability, and sometimes death. Many families cannot afford the cost of urgent surgery, and hospitals often have limited resources. Our organization exists to bridge that gap and give every child a fighting chance.
